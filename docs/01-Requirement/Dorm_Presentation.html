📋 The Problem

Why does XMUM need Dorm?

Although students spend years studying and living together on campus, communication, information sharing, club activities, second-hand trading, and community interaction remain scattered across multiple disconnected platforms.

  • Events are hard to discoverAnnouncements scattered across emails, WeChat groups, and posters — no unified entry point
  • Interest-based connections are difficultStudents struggle to find like-minded peers across faculties and year groups
  • No trusted marketplace for studentsSecond-hand trading lacks a secure, campus-only environment
  • Club management is inefficientRecruitment, event promotion, and member communication rely on multiple fragmented tools
  • Campus information is siloedCanteen guides, timetables, and handbooks lack a unified digital channel

    • 🎯 Our Vision

    Building the Digital Community Infrastructure for XMUM

    Just as physical facilities — libraries, lecture halls, and student centers — support campus life offline,
    Dorm provides the digital infrastructure that supports campus life online.

    🌐 Connect

    Break down barriers between faculties, year groups, and dormitories. Give every student a sense of belonging in a unified digital space.

    🚀 Empower

    Equip student organizations with modern digital tools — making event promotion, member management, and community operations more efficient.
