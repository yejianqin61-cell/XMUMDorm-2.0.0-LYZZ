# Scoring Algorithm 评分算法说明

---

# English

This document describes how **dish (product) scores**, **shop scores**, and **rankings** are computed in the Dorm Canteen system. Only **top-level reviews** (direct ratings on a product) are counted; replies to reviews are not.

## 1. Rating Tiers and Point Values

When leaving a top-level review, the user must choose one of five tiers. Each tier has a **fixed point value**:

| Tier | Points |
|------|--------|
| 夯爆了 (Amazing) | 10 |
| 顶级 (Top-tier) | 7 |
| 人上人 (Solid) | 4 |
| NPC | 1 |
| 拉完了 (Pass) | -1 |

## 2. Dish Composite Score (S_dish)

- **Meaning**: The composite score of a single dish (product), shown on the product page and used in rankings such as “Top Dishes” and “Hot New Items”.
- **Scope**: All **top-level reviews** for that product (replies are excluded).
- **Formula**:
  - Count reviews in each tier: \( n_1 \) (夯爆了), \( n_2 \) (顶级), \( n_3 \) (人上人), \( n_4 \) (NPC), \( n_5 \) (拉完了).
  - Total reviews: \( N = n_1 + n_2 + n_3 + n_4 + n_5 \).
  - Weighted sum:  
    \( \text{sum} = 10 n_1 + 7 n_2 + 4 n_3 + 1 n_4 + (-1) n_5 \)
  - Composite score:  
    \( S_{\text{dish}} = \dfrac{\text{sum}}{N} \)  
    Rounded to two decimal places.
- **Edge case**: If the product has no top-level reviews (\( N = 0 \)), the score is **null** (the product is excluded from score-based rankings).

## 3. Shop Composite Score (S_shop)

- **Meaning**: The composite score of a shop, used in rankings such as “Top Shops”.
- **Scope**: All products under that shop that have **at least one top-level review** (\( \text{review\_count} > 0 \)).
- **Formula**: Weighted average of product scores by review count:
  - For each product with reviews, take its \( S_{\text{dish}} \) and \( \text{review\_count} \).
  - \( S_{\text{shop}} = \dfrac{\sum (S_{\text{dish}} \times \text{review\_count})}{\sum \text{review\_count}} \)  
    Rounded to two decimal places.
- **Edge case**: If no product under the shop has any review, the shop score is **null** (excluded from score-based rankings).

## 4. Weekly Review Counts (for weekly rankings)

- **Shop weekly count**: Total number of **top-level reviews** received in the current week across all products of that shop (used in “Busiest Shops” etc.).
- **User weekly count**: Total number of **top-level reviews** posted by that user in the current week (used in “Review Stars” etc.).
- **Reset**: Every **Monday 00:00 (UTC+8)**, weekly counts for all shops and users are reset to zero and then accumulated again.

## 5. Rankings and How They Are Computed

| Ranking | Sort by | Notes |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Top Dishes (最夯单品) | \( S_{\text{dish}} \) descending; tie-break: earlier listing first | Top 5 by composite score; no reviews ⇒ excluded |
| Busiest Shops (门庭若市) | Shop weekly review count descending | Top 5 by weekly count |
| Top Shops (最夯商家) | \( S_{\text{shop}} \) descending; tie-break: earlier creation first | Top 5 by shop score; no reviews ⇒ excluded |
| Hot New Items (爆款新品) | \( S_{\text{dish}} \) descending for products listed within 7 days | Products listed ≤7 days (UTC+8); then by score |
| Review Stars (点评达人) | User weekly review count descending | Top 5 by weekly top-level review count |

## 6. Summary

- Only **top-level reviews** and their **tier** affect scores; replies do not.
- Dish score = weighted average of tier points by count; shop score = weighted average of dish scores by review count.
- Weekly rankings use **weekly review counts**, reset every Monday 00:00 UTC+8.

This document will be updated when the algorithm changes.

# 中文

本文说明食堂系统中**商品综合评分**、**店铺综合评分**及**排行榜**的计算方式。仅**一级点评**（直接对商品打分的那一条）参与统计，二级回复不参与。

## 一、点评档位与分值

用户对菜品进行一级点评时，须选择以下五档之一，每档对应一个**固定分值**：

| 档位 | 分值 |
|------|------|
| 夯爆了 | 10 |
| 顶级 | 7 |
| 人上人 | 4 |
| NPC | 1 |
| 拉完了 | -1 |

## 二、商品综合评分 S_dish

- **含义**：单个菜品（商品）的综合评分，用于商品详情页展示及「最夯单品」「爆款新品」等榜单。
- **统计范围**：该商品下所有**一级点评**（不含二级回复）。
- **计算公式**：
  - 先统计各档位数量：\( n_1 \)（夯爆了）、\( n_2 \)（顶级）、\( n_3 \)（人上人）、\( n_4 \)（NPC）、\( n_5 \)（拉完了）。
  - 总点评数：\( N = n_1 + n_2 + n_3 + n_4 + n_5 \)。
  - 加权总分：  
    \( \text{sum} = 10 n_1 + 7 n_2 + 4 n_3 + 1 n_4 + (-1) n_5 \)
  - 综合评分：  
    \( S_{\text{dish}} = \dfrac{\text{sum}}{N} \)  
    结果保留两位小数（四舍五入）。
- **特殊情况**：若该商品尚无任何一级点评（\( N = 0 \)），则综合评分记为**空**（不参与按评分排序的榜单）。

## 三、店铺综合评分 S_shop

- **含义**：单个店铺的综合评分，用于「最夯商家」等榜单。
- **统计范围**：该店铺下所有**已有一级点评**的商品（\( \text{review\_count} > 0 \)）。
- **计算公式**：按**点评数加权**的商品综合评分平均值：
  - 对店铺内每个有点评的商品，取其 \( S_{\text{dish}} \) 与 \( \text{review\_count} \)。
  - \( S_{\text{shop}} = \dfrac{\sum (S_{\text{dish}} \times \text{review\_count})}{\sum \text{review\_count}} \)  
    结果保留两位小数。
- **特殊情况**：若店铺下没有任何商品有点评，则店铺综合评分为**空**（不参与按评分排序的榜单）。

## 四、当周点评数（周榜用）

- **店铺当周点评数**：该店铺下所有商品在一周内收到的**一级点评**总数，用于「门庭若市」等周榜。
- **用户当周点评数**：该用户在一周内发表的**一级点评**总数，用于「点评达人」等周榜。
- **重置规则**：每周一 **0:00（东八区）** 自动将全部店铺与用户的当周点评数清零，重新累计。

## 五、排行榜与算法对应关系

| 榜单 | 排序依据 | 说明 |
|------|----------|------|
| 最夯单品 | 商品 \( S_{\text{dish}} \) 降序，同分按上架时间早的在前 | 累计综合评分 Top 5，零点评不参与 |
| 门庭若市 | 店铺当周点评数降序 | 当周点评数 Top 5 |
| 最夯商家 | 店铺 \( S_{\text{shop}} \) 降序，同分按创建时间早的在前 | 店铺综合评分 Top 5，零点评不参与 |
| 爆款新品 | 上架 7 天内商品的 \( S_{\text{dish}} \) 降序 | 东八区「上架距今 ≤7 天」且参与评分排序 |
| 点评达人 | 用户当周点评数降序 | 当周一级点评数 Top 5 |

## 六、小结

- 评分只与**一级点评**的**档位**有关，二级回复不参与任何分数计算。
- 商品分 = 各档位分值按条数加权平均；店铺分 = 旗下商品分按点评数加权平均。
- 周榜依赖「当周点评数」，每周一 0 点东八区重置。

如有变更会同步更新此说明。
